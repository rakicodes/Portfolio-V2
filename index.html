Hi I'm Raquel! I'm a front-end web developer based in Edmonton, Alberta. I make responsive websites and web applications.

Need a website?

When someone comes to your website, will they have a good experience? How will the site look if they reach it on a phone? What about a tablet? Or a laptop?
In this day and age a responsive and accessible website is a must have for any business. That is where I come in, I can help you build a new website or update your old website.

I will provide you with a consultation where we can talk about your goals for the website and how I can help you achieve them. Then I will create a visual representation of the website to make sure my understanding of your goals is met early on in the process. After this, I will begin to make your website where you will receive regular updates on my progress to ensure the website matches your expectations. Finally, once the website is finished, we launch! After the launch, if you require ongoing support I also provide a variety of support services including monitoring and optimization of my completed websites.
